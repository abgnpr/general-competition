# RBI Grade B — Recommended Books & Resources

## Quantitative Aptitude
- **R.S. Aggarwal** — *Quantitative Aptitude for Competitive Examinations* (basics + practice)
- **Arun Sharma** — *How to Prepare for Quantitative Aptitude for CAT* (harder problems, better problem-solving approach)

## Reasoning
- **R.S. Aggarwal** — *Verbal and Non-Verbal Reasoning* (core topics)
- **Arun Sharma** — *How to Prepare for Logical Reasoning* (analytical thinking, tricky questions)

## English
- **S.P. Bakshi** — *Objective General English* (practice + grammar)
- **Norman Lewis** — *Word Power Made Easy* (vocabulary)
- **Wren & Martin** — *High School Grammar and Composition* (grammar fundamentals)

## General Awareness
No single book covers everything. Focus on **current affairs from the last 5–6 months** before the exam.

## ESI (Economic and Social Issues)
- **Uma Kapila** — *Indian Economy*
- **Debraj Ray** — *Development Economics* (Growth and Development)
- **NCERT Economics** — Class 11 & 12

## Finance and Management
- **Prasanna Chandra** — *Financial Management*
- **L.M. Prasad** — *Principles and Practice of Management*
