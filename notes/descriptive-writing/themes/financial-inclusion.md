## Key Data Points

- Jan Dhan accounts (latest): ~52 crore
- Jan Dhan deposits: ~₹2.3 lakh crore
- PMJDY women account share: ~55%
- RuPay cards issued: ~37 crore
- Priority Sector Lending target: 40% of ANBC

- JAM trinity (Jan Dhan + Aadhaar + Mobile)
- Mudra Yojana (PMMY) — Shishu / Kishor / Tarun
- Stand-Up India
- Nachiket Mor Committee (2014) — small finance banks, payments banks
- PSL guidelines (RBI)
- Financial Inclusion Index (RBI) — 64.2 (FY24)
