# Cloze Test

**Tier 1 · Priority 2.** Vocabulary + grammar + context. Highly trainable.

Status: not started

## Concept Map

<!-- Cloze is testing: does the word FIT grammatically and semantically? -->

## Approach

1. Read the full passage once (ignore blanks) to get the topic and tone.
2. For each blank: parse the sentence → what part of speech fits? → what meaning fits the context?
3. Options that are grammatically wrong → eliminate first.
4. Then narrow on meaning. Tone is the final filter.
