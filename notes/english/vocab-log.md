# Vocabulary Log

Daily capture — 10 words from editorial reading or RC passages. **Not** from random word lists.

## Format

```text
Date | Word | Meaning (your own words) | Sentence from source | Source
```

Keep meanings short and in *your* words — dictionary definitions don't stick.
