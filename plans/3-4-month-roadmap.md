# 3-4 Month Common Roadmap

RBI Grade B-centric, SBI & IBPS kept fully alive.

## Month 1: Foundation + Habit Building

**Goal:** Concepts over speed. Build reading and GA muscle early.

### Quantitative Aptitude

- Percentages, Ratio & Proportion
- Averages, Time & Work
- Basics of DI (tables, bar graphs)
- Focus: understanding, not timers yet

### Reasoning Ability

- Syllogism (new pattern)
- Inequality
- Blood relations
- Direction sense
- Intro to seating arrangements

### English

- Reading comprehension daily (short passages)
- Grammar basics: subject-verb, tenses, prepositions

### GA / Economics (light but daily)

- Inflation, GDP, Repo rate, CPI vs WPI
- Daily news reading (30 min)

**Outcome:** You start thinking like an RBI aspirant, without losing PO basics.

## Month 2: Expansion + Structure

**Goal:** Cover full syllabus once, start light speed work.

### Quant

- Number series
- Time, speed & distance
- Profit & loss
- Mixed DI sets

### Reasoning

- Floor-based & circular seating
- Puzzles (moderate level)
- Coding-decoding

### English

- RCs (longer passages)
- Cloze test
- Error spotting

### Economics & Finance

- Monetary policy tools
- Banking system in India
- Financial markets basics

### GA

- Start monthly current affairs notes
- Revise weekly

**Outcome:** PO-level questions start feeling familiar; RBI syllabus stops looking alien.

## Month 3: Integration + Mocks

**Goal:** Merge speed with depth.

### Quant & Reasoning

- Timed sectional tests (2-3 per week)
- Mixed practice sets

### English

- RC + grammar under time
- Begin descriptive answer structure (RBI)

### RBI Core

- Finance & Management concepts
- Economic issues linked to current affairs

### GA

- Last 2-3 months CA revision
- Banking awareness MCQs

**Outcome:** You can switch between "fast banker mode" and "policy brain mode".

## Month 4 (if available): Exam-Tuning Mode

**Goal:** Adapt to whichever exam notification drops first.

- Full-length mocks
- Descriptive practice (RBI Grade B)
- High-frequency GA revision
- Computer awareness (quick win)
