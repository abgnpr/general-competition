# Books & Resources (Lean List)

One subject, one book, one current affairs source. Anything extra must replace, not add.

## Quantitative Aptitude

- **Quantitative Aptitude for Competitive Exams** — R.S. Aggarwal
- Use selectively. Don't solve cover to cover.

## Reasoning

- **A Modern Approach to Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning** — R.S. Aggarwal
- Focus on puzzles, syllogism, inequality.

## English

- **Word Power Made Easy** — Norman Lewis
- **Objective General English** — S.P. Bakshi
- Free add-on: Editorials from The Hindu or Indian Express

## Economics & Finance (RBI Core)

- **Indian Economy** — Ramesh Singh (selected chapters only)
- **Finance & Management** — RBI Grade B guide (any one standard edition)
- Free: RBI website reports, Budget & Economic Survey summaries

## Current Affairs

- ONE monthly PDF source only (choose one and stick):
  - VisionIAS Monthly
  - InsightsIAS Monthly
  - AffairsCloud Monthly
- Supplement with daily 30-min newspaper reading + self-made micro-notes
- Avoid: multiple apps, multiple PDFs, Telegram overload

## Operating Rule

One subject, one book, one current affairs source. Anything extra must **replace**, not add.
