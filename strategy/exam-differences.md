# Exam Differences

## Big-Picture Snapshot

| Feature | IBPS PO | SBI PO | RBI Grade B |
|---------|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Conducting Body** | IBPS | State Bank of India | Reserve Bank of India |
| **Role Type** | Commercial banking | Commercial banking | Central banking & policy |
| **Competition Level** | High | Very high | Extremely high |
| **Transfers** | Frequent | Frequent | Rare |
| **Prestige** | High | High | Very high |
| **Work-Life Balance** | Medium | Medium | Good |
| **Starting Salary (approx)** | ~55-60k/month | ~65-70k/month | ~80-90k/month |

## Pattern Comparison

| Feature | IBPS PO | SBI PO | RBI Grade B |
|---------|---------|--------|-------------|
| **Prelims** | Quant, Reasoning, English | Quant, Reasoning, English | Quant, Reasoning, English, GA |
| **Mains** | Reasoning+Computer, English, Quant, GA/Economy | Reasoning+Computer, English, Data Analysis, GA/Economy | Economic & Social Issues, Finance & Management |
| **Descriptive** | Letter/Essay | Letter/Essay | Paper III (Finance & Management) |
| **Interview** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Sectional Cutoff** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Negative Marking** | 0.25 per wrong | 0.25 per wrong | 0.25 per wrong |

## Difficulty Ladder

IBPS PO -> SBI PO -> RBI Grade B

But remember: difficulty != suitability.

## Exam Personalities

### IBPS PO — "Grow Steadily"

- More pattern-based questions
- Slightly easier than SBI PO
- Interviews are structured and calm
- Steady promotions, pressure varies bank-to-bank
- **Best for:** candidates seeking balance between competition and comfort

### SBI PO — "Lead from the Front"

- Heavy on reasoning + DI, questions often feel unpredictable
- Interview panel is famously grilling
- Early responsibility, fast promotions
- Transfers across India are common
- **Best for:** ambitious, energetic candidates who thrive under pressure

### RBI Grade B — "Think Nationally"

- Objective + descriptive papers
- Focus: Economics, Finance & Management, current affairs with depth
- Speed matters less, conceptual clarity matters more
- Policy formulation, regulation, research — mostly metro postings
- **Best for:** academically inclined, patient, strategy-focused aspirants

## Overlapping Syllabus

~70-80% of preparation is shared across all three exams (Quant, Reasoning, English, GA). See [common-subject-base.md](common-subject-base.md) for the full breakdown.
